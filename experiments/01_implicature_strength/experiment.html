During this experiment you will alternate back and forth between two tasks:
Half of the time you will be prompted with an arithmetic equation. Your task is to find and provide the answer to the provided equation to the best of your ability. You will be paid even if you make math mistakes and you may use a pen and paper to help. However, please DO NOT use a calculator.
The other half of the time you will be prompted with a sentence that has been cut short. For example "The student was absent from school because she _____ ." Your task is to fill in the blank to complete the provided sentence.
The experiment will take approximately 30 minutes.
